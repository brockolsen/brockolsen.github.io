Johnson Design is a family owned business started in the winter of 2016. Sara has always loved getting ready and setting up for parties or family events. She has always dreamed of starting her own event planning service and is ready and willing to take on any challenge big or small. In an ever increasingly busy world people have less and less time to prepare for the important moments in life such as Weddings or anniversaries.

At Johnson Design we care about people and we know how important this day is that you have been planning for. Our goal and focus is to help you have an amazing time and that when you have another event in mind your first thoughts will be to turn to us for help because we are there for you.
