Event Planning

Located at the heart of Eastern Idaho Johnson Design is your go to service for all of you event planning and catering needs. We are professionals interested in helping you prepare every needful thing to help you and those you invite have a wonderful time at your next company or family event. We specialize in everything from preparing and handing out invitations to making an amazing and delicious meal for your valued guests. Each event is personalized to meet your needs and reach the goals that you set.

Here at Johnson Design we work with individuals to and prepare them to have a unique experience at any time of the year. You are guaranteed a first-class experience at the event and will absolutely love the arrangement of decorations and quality design principles. We help you prepare for any size event and love being able to see your dream become a reality.

We will help make sure your program is covered in a wide variety of ways from quality decorations to experienced photographers, we can light up you program with lighting and live music, and provide ideas on locations that you would have never thought of. At many events we have amazing giveaways and provide spectacular prizes.
